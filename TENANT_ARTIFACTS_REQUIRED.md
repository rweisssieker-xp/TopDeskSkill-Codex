# Tenant Artifacts Required

The skill suite is generic until tenant-specific TOPdesk artifacts are provided.

## Minimum Useful Bundle

- TOPdesk OData `$metadata` XML.
- OData service document.
- 20-50 sample incidents.
- Category, status, priority, branch, operator group exports.
- Existing report/PBIX screenshots or DAX/M snippets.
- KPI definitions used in management reporting.

## Recommended Full Bundle

- API samples for incidents, persons, operators, branches, changes, assets, knowledge items.
- CSV exports from trusted TOPdesk selections/reports.
- SSP form screenshots/configuration notes.
- Change and asset template examples.
- Existing Power BI model documentation.
- Historical resolved tickets with final category/group/resolution for AI evaluation.
- Security/RLS requirements by branch, customer, or team.

## Expected Outputs After Artifact Review

- Verified field catalog.
- Tenant-specific local schema mapping.
- Power BI fact/dimension mapping.
- RLS mapping.
- Data-quality findings.
- KPI reconciliation matrix.
- AI evaluation dataset plan.
