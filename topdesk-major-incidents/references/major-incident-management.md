# TOPdesk Major Incident Management

Use this file for major incident detection, escalation, communication, war-room coordination, linking child incidents, and postmortems.

## Detection Signals

- Spike in similar incidents by service, asset, branch, error code, or time window.
- Monitoring alert linked to multiple user reports.
- VIP/business-critical service affected.
- SLA breach risk across many incidents.
- Duplicate detection finds many related tickets.

## Workflow

1. Identify candidate major incident.
2. Confirm scope, affected service, start time, and business impact.
3. Assign major incident owner and technical lead.
4. Link related incidents.
5. Start communication cadence.
6. Track workaround, resolution, and closure.
7. Run postmortem and knowledge update.

## Communication Cadence

- Initial acknowledgement.
- Impact and workaround update.
- Regular status updates.
- Resolution confirmation.
- Post-incident summary.

## KPIs

- Time to detect.
- Time to communicate.
- Time to workaround.
- Time to resolve.
- Number of linked incidents.
- Reopen/recurrence count.
- Postmortem action completion.
