# Rollout Handbook

Audience: project leads, change managers, application managers, and service leads.

## Rollout Phases

1. Discovery and artifact collection.
2. Tenant mapping and schema design.
3. Power BI MVP.
4. Data-quality cleanup.
5. AI suggest-only pilot.
6. Training and adoption.
7. Controlled automation.
8. Continuous improvement.

## Cutover Checklist

- Final sync completed.
- Reports reconciled.
- RLS tested.
- Operators trained.
- Runbooks ready.
- Rollback/forward-fix plan approved.
- Business owner signs off.

## Adoption Metrics

- Report usage.
- Training completion.
- AI acceptance rate.
- Override reasons.
- Reassignment rate.
- Data-quality issue trend.
- SLA trend.
