# Training Handbook

Audience: trainers, team leads, service desk leads, and onboarding owners.

## Training Tracks

Operators:

- Ticket handling.
- Public/internal notes.
- AI suggestions.
- Knowledge links.
- Closure quality.

Leads:

- Power BI dashboards.
- SLA and backlog.
- AI adoption.
- Data quality.

Application managers:

- Configuration governance.
- Mapping impact.
- Report validation.
- Workflow changes.

## 60-Minute Operator Session

1. Overview and goals.
2. Ticket workflow.
3. AI suggestions and review.
4. Knowledge and draft replies.
5. Escalation and major incidents.
6. Common mistakes.
7. Q&A.

## Training Evidence

- Attendance.
- Scenario completion.
- Feedback.
- Follow-up issues.
