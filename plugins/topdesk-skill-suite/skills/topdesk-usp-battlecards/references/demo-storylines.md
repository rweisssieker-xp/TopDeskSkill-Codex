- Before: exports, manual joins, unclear SLA risk, no AI audit.
- After: tenant-mapped model, governed AI suggestions, Power BI cockpit, ROI view.

2. Show mapped TOPdesk model.
3. Show Power BI KPI and drill-through.
4. Show AI suggestion with confidence and audit.
5. Show governance cockpit.
6. Close with ROI and rollout path.
