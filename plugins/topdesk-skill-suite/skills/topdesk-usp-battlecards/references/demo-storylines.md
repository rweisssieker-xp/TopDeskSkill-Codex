# Demo Storylines

## Before And After

- Before: exports, manual joins, current-state reporting, unclear SLA risk, hidden waiting time, no AI audit.
- After: tenant-mapped model, lifecycle history, daily snapshots, governed AI suggestions, Power BI cockpit, reconciliation, runbook evidence, ROI view.

## Demo Flow

1. Show data-quality or reporting pain.
2. Show mapped TOPdesk model.
3. Show Power BI KPI and drill-through from executive summary to incident timeline.
4. Show lifecycle proof: status duration, operator-group duration, reassignment, and snapshot backlog trend.
5. Show reconciliation: TOPdesk count, Power BI count, difference, and explanation.
6. Show AI suggestion with confidence, source, permission scope, feedback, and audit.
7. Show operations evidence: runbook, health report, package validation, and checksum.
8. Close with five-day proof path, ROI, and rollout path.

## Executive Control Storyline

Before:

- Leadership sees ticket totals but cannot explain why backlog or SLA risk changed.
- Status and group waiting time are discussed anecdotally.
- Reporting disputes consume time because KPI definitions are unclear.

After:

- A management KPI drills down into affected team, category, status, group, and incident lifecycle.
- Bottlenecks are ranked by average/P90 duration and linked to routing or configuration actions.
- KPI definitions and reconciliation evidence are visible.

Demo flow:

1. Open executive cockpit with backlog, SLA risk, waiting time, reassignment, and improvement priority.
2. Select a high-risk group or category.
3. Drill into status/group duration and daily snapshot trend.
4. Open one incident timeline with `ValidFrom`, `ValidTo`, duration, and sequence.
5. Close with concrete action: routing change, category cleanup, knowledge work, or AI assist pilot.

## Five-Day PoV Storyline

Day 1:

- Show KPI baseline and trusted TOPdesk counts.

Day 2:

- Show tenant mapping, data-quality findings, and first reconciliation.

Day 3:

- Show lifecycle bottleneck dashboard and snapshot trend.

Day 4:

- Show governance proof: AI audit, RLS/security, runbook, or change-readiness check.

Day 5:

- Show ROI assumptions, risks, data gaps, and go/no-go roadmap.

## No-Lock-In Storyline

- Show the same metric across source CSV/API evidence, normalized fact table, Power Query import, DAX measure, and Power BI visual.
- Explain that the customer owns the logic in reviewable artifacts instead of a black-box reporting platform.
- Close with package verification and checksum evidence for release confidence.
