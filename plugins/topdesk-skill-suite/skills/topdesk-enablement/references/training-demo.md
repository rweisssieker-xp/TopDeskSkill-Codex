# Training, Demo, and Enablement

Use this file for demos, onboarding, training plans, user guides, role-based instructions, and adoption material for TOPdesk apps, reports, integrations, and AI/KI features.

## Audience Tracks

Operators:

- Incident intake and classification.
- Public vs internal notes.
- AI suggestion review.
- Knowledge lookup and draft reply handling.
- Reopen and closure rules.

Service desk leads:

- Backlog and SLA dashboard interpretation.
- Reassignment and category-quality monitoring.
- AI acceptance and override monitoring.
- Data-quality ownership.

Application managers:

- Category/status/supporting-file governance.
- OData/API mapping ownership.
- Power BI refresh and reconciliation.
- Workflow, SSP form, and action sequence changes.

Security/compliance:

- PII handling.
- RLS and branch visibility.
- AI context boundaries.
- Audit evidence and retention.

## Demo Script

1. Show an incoming incident.
2. Show suggested category, assignment group, and confidence.
3. Show operator accepting/editing the suggestion.
4. Show AI-generated handoff summary and draft reply.
5. Show knowledge citation or similar incident.
6. Show schema/audit record for suggestion and feedback.
7. Show Power BI impact: backlog, SLA, reassignment, AI acceptance.
8. Show governance: RLS, audit, and disable switch.

## Training Module Outline

Module 1: TOPdesk data basics

- Incidents, changes, assets, persons, operators, branches, categories.
- Why consistent fields matter for reporting and AI.

Module 2: Working with improved workflows

- SSP forms, routing, required fields, public/internal communication.

Module 3: Power BI reports

- KPI definitions, slicers, drill-through, RLS, refresh timestamps.

Module 4: AI/KI assist

- What AI can suggest.
- What requires human review.
- How to provide feedback.
- What not to paste into prompts or notes.

Module 5: Support and escalation

- Reporting mismatch.
- Failed sync.
- Incorrect AI suggestion.
- Permission issue.

## Quick Reference Card

Operator:

- Review confidence and reason before accepting AI suggestions.
- Keep internal notes out of customer-visible replies.
- Use knowledge citations when sending guidance.
- Override wrong category/routing and choose a reason.

Lead:

- Check backlog, SLA risk, reassignment, and AI acceptance weekly.
- Investigate categories with high reopen or reassignment.
- Review data-quality page before KPI meetings.

Application manager:

- Keep categories and operator groups clean.
- Verify OData/API mapping after TOPdesk configuration changes.
- Validate reports after schema or workflow changes.

## Adoption Metrics

- Active report users.
- Report refresh success.
- Training completion.
- AI suggestion acceptance rate.
- Override reason distribution.
- Reduction in reassignment.
- Reduction in missing category/branch/caller fields.
- Operator feedback sentiment.
