# TOPdesk SLA Optimizer

Use this skill when a tenant snapshot or export should become operational SLA actions.

## Workflow

1. Identify the incident grain and available date/status/priority/operator-group fields.
2. Separate missing-data risk from true SLA or backlog risk.
3. Bucket incidents by status, priority, operator group, target date, and ageing where fields exist.
4. Produce action lists: missing targets, weak routing, high-priority backlog, stale open work, and overloaded groups.
5. Feed the outputs into Power BI measures, operational reviews, and AI routing suggestions.

## Scripts

- Use `scripts/analyze_sla_backlog.py` against `snapshots/incidents.json` generated by tenant mapping.
