# Executive Handbook

Audience: sponsors, IT leaders, service owners, and management.

## Value Proposition

The TOPdesk solution connects operational workflows, tenant-verified data mapping, Power BI analytics, AI/KI assistance, and governance into one measurable service-management improvement program.

## Outcomes

- Better SLA visibility.
- Reduced triage and reassignment effort.
- Trusted Power BI reporting.
- Safer AI-assisted workflows.
- Improved data quality.
- Stronger audit and compliance.
- Better self-service and knowledge reuse.

## Decision Points

- Scope and priority.
- Tenant data access.
- Security and AI boundaries.
- KPI definitions.
- Rollout phase approval.
- Success metrics.

## Executive KPIs

- SLA compliance.
- Backlog trend.
- First response time.
- Resolution time.
- Reassignment rate.
- Deflection rate.
- AI acceptance rate.
- Reporting reconciliation delta.
- Data-quality issue trend.

## Roadmap

1. Data foundation.
2. Power BI reporting.
3. Workflow quality.
4. AI suggest-only.
5. Controlled automation.
6. Continuous improvement.
